A new, reliable way to play chess.

Ever wanted to play on lichess and not be distracted by anything else? With our Enhanced Silence feature, volumes are changed inside the lichess menu.

Scared of phishing and malware through links? Lichess+ will never change the site you are on from lichess to something else, but will open it in your reccomended browser, making malware easily identifiable.

Browser extensions are sometimes quite dodgy, but on Lichess+ these will always be disabled to enhance fair play.

Is your FPS too slow? We can boost it for you with this client.

What's best? It will always be free, forever.

Lichess+ Client is still in its beta. Bug reports can go to @LichessPlus on lichess.
